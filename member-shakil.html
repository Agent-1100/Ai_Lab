About

I completed my Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science and Engineering from Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST) in 2023. After finishing my undergraduate studies, I enrolled in the Master of Science (M.Sc.) program in Computer Science and Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET), where I am currently continuing my studies. Since August 2023, I have been working as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Varendra University, Rajshahi. Alongside my academic progression, I have been involved in research work since 2023 and have published several articles. My areas of interest include image processing, audio signal processing, deep learning, and machine learning. In my teaching role, I have been engaged in conducting both theoretical and laboratory courses. The courses I have taught or am currently teaching include Object-Oriented Programming and Object-Oriented Programming Lab, Object-Oriented Design and Design Patterns along with its lab component, Parallel Processing and Distributed Systems and its lab, and E-commerce and Web Programming with its lab sessions. I have also taken courses on Software Engineering, Database Management System Lab, and Digital Image Processing Lab. My current focus is on continuing my postgraduate studies, carrying out research activities, and fulfilling my responsibilities as a lecturer. I aim to further develop my academic and research work in the coming years.
