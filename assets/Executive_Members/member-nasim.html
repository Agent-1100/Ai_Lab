About

I am Md. Farhan Tanvir Nasim, a Mechatronics Engineer and current M.Sc. student at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). I completed my B.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering from RUET in 2024. I previously worked as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Varendra University, Rajshahi, where I taught undergraduate courses and conducted laboratory sessions. My research interests include renewable energy systems, microgrid energy management, machine learning, and blockchain-based smart energy solutions. I have worked on projects such as IoT-based plant disease detection, real-time patient monitoring, and microgrid system design using MATLAB. I am skilled in C, MATLAB, PLC programming, embedded systems, and design tools like AutoCAD and SOLIDWORKS, with a strong focus on solving real-world engineering problems.
