About

I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Varendra University, Bangladesh, in 2021, and completed my M.Sc. in the same field at Islamic University, Kushtia, Bangladesh. At present, I am working as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Varendra University, Rajshahi, Bangladesh. My research interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Image Processing with a particular emphasis on image classification and transformer-based approaches. Alongside my research activities, I am actively involved in teaching undergraduate courses, including Algorithms, Software Engineering, System Analysis, and Computer Fundamentals. For any academic or professional inquiries, I can be contacted at jamilchaudhary07@gmail.com
