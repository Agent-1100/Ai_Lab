Join the Lab

Choose the role that fits how you want to contribute.

We welcome researchers, mentors, and learners who want to contribute to rigorous, collaborative, and impact-driven AI research.

Contributor

For active collaborators who want to work directly on projects, writing, and prototypes.

  • Hands-on involvement in active research projects
  • Opportunity to co-author papers and reports
  • Access to internal brainstorming and review sessions
  • Participation in prototype development and testing
  • Recognition on project and publication pages
  • Skill-building through collaborative research work

Advisor

For senior academics and experts who want to guide strategy, quality, and direction.

  • Advisory role in research planning and direction
  • Opportunity to mentor contributors and student members
  • Input into publication quality and scholarly framing
  • Invitation to strategic discussions and milestone reviews
  • Visibility as a formal lab advisor
  • Collaboration opportunities across themes and institutions

Student Member

For students looking to learn, contribute, and grow in a research-focused environment.

  • Structured exposure to real research workflows
  • Learning support in literature review and scientific writing
  • Participation in discussions, reading groups, and meetings
  • Opportunity to assist with benchmarks, data, and experiments
  • Mentorship from lab seniors and advisors
  • Portfolio-building through meaningful project experience
