About

I am Md. Rakibul Islam, currently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Varendra University, Bangladesh. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology in 2024. I am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at the same institution. My research interests include machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and biomedical signal processing, with a strong focus on applying intelligent techniques to healthcare and real-world problem solving. As a lecturer, I am actively involved in delivering outcome-based education (OBE)-aligned courses, conducting laboratory sessions, and supervising undergraduate projects and theses. I strive to create an engaging learning environment by emphasizing hands-on practice, critical thinking, and real-world application of theoretical concepts. I have worked on several academic and practical projects. One of my notable projects is a Bangla Sign Language Interpreter, designed to assist the hearing-impaired community by translating gestures into understandable language using embedded systems and machine learning techniques. I am proficient in programming languages such as Python, C/C++, Java, and MATLAB, and have experience working with modern tools and frameworks including TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and computer vision libraries. My technical skill set also includes simulation tools, database systems, and web technologies, enabling me to work across interdisciplinary domains.
